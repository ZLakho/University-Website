Chairman Message

The Department of Computer System Engineering is the fast-growing department at Dawood University of Engineering & Technology. The key to success of CSE department lies in the aspirations of our students, the hard work of our faculty members and the support of DUET leadership. These factors provide us confidence and believe as we target to achieve the next level of excellence.The field of computer science and engineering is growing at an exponential rate. We offer a variety of different degree programs designed as per industrial requirements. We have diverse faculty having specializations in Software Engineering, Web Engineering, Mobile Application Development, Machine Learning, Data Science, Wireless Communication, Embedded Systems. I invite prospective students to visit us and join us to explore latest technologies and develop skills to face the challenges and create solutions for the betterment of the society.

The passed out students start their role in the progress of the nation after getting jobs in reputed organizations in the various and connected field of Computers, some of students pursuing the higher studies as well.
